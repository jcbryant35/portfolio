Flying Tips Website

Flying tips is an informational website for first time flyers. It was my first website to be published on the internet.

I was inspired to create this website because the first time I ever flew on a plane I was solo. After that experience, I decided to create a simple website with tips and how to's in hopes to help someone else in the world who may be in the same situation.
