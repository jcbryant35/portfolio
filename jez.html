Jez Variety Trend Website

Jez Variety Trend is my second published website, it's a products display website. It was created when a client reached out to me to help them simplify their products advertising.

Before reaching out to me, the client explained they were just starting up their business and listing their products on social media daily and instead they wanted to have a website to display all of their products and pricing so they could just share one link to customers and be able to view all of their products in one place.

What's next?


The client has recently contacted me about the success they have had with the products display website.

We are currently discussing turning it into a full-blown e-commerce store with a shopping cart, an online payment system, shipping system, and create user login accounts.

There will also be a dashboard for admins to add/edit products and pricing, and keep track of sales and inventory. It will also undergo a slight design change.
